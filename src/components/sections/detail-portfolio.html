Project Overview

This comprehensive electrical installation project involved designing and implementing a complete electrical infrastructure for a modern 15-story office building in Jakarta's central business district. The project required careful planning, coordination with multiple stakeholders, and adherence to the highest safety and quality standards.

Our team successfully delivered a state-of-the-art electrical system that includes smart lighting controls, energy-efficient power distribution, emergency backup systems, and integrated building management capabilities. The project was completed on time and within budget, exceeding client expectations.

Technical Specifications

Power System

  • 2.5MW total capacity
  • Redundant power feeds
  • UPS backup system
  • Emergency generators

Lighting System

  • Smart controls & sensors

Safety Systems
